Research

My research interests include data mining, unsupervised learning, semi-supervised learning and graph-based machine learning, especially the expressive power and generalizability of Graph Neural Networks. I also have a broad interest in trustworthy machine learning and artificial intelligence for science-related areas, such as using Graph Neural Networks for drug discovery or for the betterment of society.
